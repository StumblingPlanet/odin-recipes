Aligot - cheese mashed potatoes with baked vegetables

Soulfood in French

You know those days when you just want to sit in the warm and feel comfortable. This dish will highlight your day. It's a mashed potato with cheese folded in and hearty baked vegetables.

Ingredients for 2 portions:
  • 20 gram butter
  • six sprigs thyme
  • two red onions
  • 60 grams of creme fraiche
  • two carrots
  • 160 gramm Tomme Käse
  • 600 gram potatoes
  • 1 red bell bell pepper
  • 1/4 celery bulb
  • Nutmeg
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • 2 spoons of olive oil
  • 1 clove garlic

  1. Preheat oven to 200°C (top and bottom heat, recommended) or 180°C (convection oven).
  2. Peel the celery and cut into small cubes
  3. Peel carrot, quarter lengthwise and cut into small pieces.
  4. Peel the onion, halve it and cut it into medium sized pieces.
  5. Wash the peppers, remove the seeds and cut into small cubes.
  6. Wash thyme, shake dry and pluck leaves.
  7. Place on a baking tray lined with baking paper, add thyme, drizzle with oil, salt, pepper and bake for about 20 minutes.
  8. Peel the potatoes and cut them into pieces.
  9. Boil potatoes with a peeled clove of garlic in salted water for about 15 minutes.
  10. Meanwhile, remove the rind from the cheese and cut into thin slices.
  11. Turn cooked potatoes into mash. Add butter and creme fraiche and mix well.
  12. Gradually add the cheese slices to the puree so that they melt and combine with the puree. Season to taste with salt, pepper and nutmeg.
  13. Enjoy aligot with vegetables.
