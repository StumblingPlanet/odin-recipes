Pasta casserole with tuna and pesto

This Pasta with tuna casserole has all you need.

A quick and easy casserole to warm your heart with tuna and pesto flavour

Ingredients for 2 portions:
  • 15 gram of parsley
  • 50 gram of Creme fraiche
  • 20 gram of Panko breadcrumbs
  • eight sprigs thyme
  • 200 gram of Cherry tomatoes
  • 120 gram of grated cheese
  • 120 gram of tuna or vegetarian equivalent
  • 160g of Fussili pasta
  • 30 gram of Pesto rosso
  • 2 spoons olive oil

  1. Cook pasta according to package directions.
  2. Wash and halve cherry tomatoes.
  3. Wash parsley, shake dry and chop coarsely.
  4. Thyme wash, shake dry and finely chop.
  5. Drain tuna and place in a large bowl. Add drained pasta, half of the cheese, tomatoes, chopped herbs, pesto, olive oil and creme fraiche and mix well. Season with salt and pepper.
  6. Put everything in a baking dish, sprinkle with remaining cheese and panko breadcrumbs and bake for 20 minutes.
  7. When time is up, remove from oven and enjoy.
