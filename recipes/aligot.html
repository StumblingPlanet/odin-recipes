Aligot

A french cuisine classic

Ingredients:

  • sprigs of fresh thyme, 12
  • red onions, 4
  • crème fraîche, 120g
  • Tomme cheese, 320g
  • potatoes, 1200g
  • red bell peppers, 2
  • celery root, 1/2
  • carrots, 4

    • Also:

  • nutmeg
  • pepper
  • salt
  • olive oil, 4 tablespoons
  • cloves garlic, 2
  • butter, 40g

Steps:
  1. Preheat the oven to 200°C (top and bottom heat, recommended) or 180°C (fan oven).
  2. Peel the celery and cut it into small cubes.
  3. Peel the carrot, quarter it lengthwise, and cut it into small pieces.
  4. Peel the onion, cut it in half, and chop it into medium-sized pieces.
  5. Wash the bell peppers, remove the seeds, and cut into small cubes.
  6. Wash the thyme, shake dry, and remove the leaves.
  7. Place on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper, sprinkle with thyme, drizzle with oil, season with salt and pepper, and bake for about 20 minutes.
  8. Peel the potatoes and cut them into pieces.
  9. Boil the potatoes with a peeled garlic clove in salted water for about 15 minutes.
  10. Meanwhile, remove the rind from the cheese and cut it into thin slices.
  11. Mash the cooked potatoes. Add butter and crème fraîche and mix well.
  12. Gradually add the cheese slices to the purée so that they melt and combine with the purée. Season to taste with salt, pepper, and nutmeg.
  13. Enjoy aligot with vegetables.
