Ingredients:

    For the Bolognese:

  • Olive oil
  • Mixed ground meat, 500g
  • Onions, 2
  • Garlic cloves, 1 bunch
  • Frozen parsley, 1 tbsp
  • Tomato paste, 1 can
  • Red wine

    • For the béchamel sauce:

  • Milk, 0.5 liter
  • Butter, 30g
  • Flour, 40g
  • Salt and pepper
  • Lemon juice
  • Nutmeg

    • Also:

  • Fat for the baking dish
  • Lasagna sheets, 300g
  • Grated cheese
  • Butter flakes

Steps:
  1. Ragú Bolognese:

    Heat the olive oil in a pot, brown the ground beef, and add the chopped onions and parsley. Stir in the finely sliced garlic and tomato paste and fry together. Pour in the canned tomatoes, season with salt and pepper, and add red wine to taste. Simmer the ragù for at least half an hour with the lid off.

  2. Béchamelsauce:

    Melt the butter in a small saucepan, stir in the flour with a whisk and sauté until light yellow. Add the milk and stir the sauce until smooth. If you have stirred too slowly and find lumps in the sauce, you can pass the sauce through a fine hair sieve and then continue cooking. The sauce should simmer on a low

  3. Preparation of the lasagna:

    Spread some ragù Bolognese in a buttered, ovenproof dish, place a layer of lasagna sheets on top, cover the pasta layer with ragù again and then with a layer of béchamel sauce. Then add another layer of pasta, ragù and béchamel sauce. Fill the dish layer by layer. The last layer should be béchamel sauce. Sprinkle generously with grated cheese and dot with butter.

  4. Bake the lasagna in a hot oven at 180 °C (fan oven) for approx. 30-40 minutes until the crust is golden brown.
