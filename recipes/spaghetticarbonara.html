Spaghetti carbonara

The classical variant of Spaghetti carbonara

Ingredients:

  • red onions, 2
  • diced bacon, 160g
  • fresh parsley, 30g
  • Pecorino cheese, 120g
  • eggs, 8
  • spaghetti, 280g

    • Also:

  • Pepper
  • Salt
  • olive oil, 2 tbsp
  • cloves of garlic, 4

Steps:
  1. Cook the pasta according to the instructions on the packet. When draining, reserve some of the cooking water.
  2. Meanwhile, grate the Pecorino cheese.
  3. Mix 4 eggs and grated Pecorino cheese.
  4. Meanwhile, peel and dice the red onions.
  5. Peel and grate the garlic.
  6. Heat oil in a pan and fry onions, garlic, and bacon for about 7 minutes.
  7. Meanwhile, separate the remaining egg.
  8. Wash the parsley, shake dry, and chop finely.
  9. Add the cooked spaghetti, some of the cooking water (approx. 200 ml), and the egg and Pecorino mixture to the pan and mix quickly. Season with salt and pepper.
  10. Remove the pan from the heat. Mix the spaghetti alla carbonara in the hot pan with the separated egg yolks and chopped parsley and serve immediately.
